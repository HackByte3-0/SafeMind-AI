How to Use SafeMinds

Follow these guidelines for accurate depression detection results

Preparation Instructions

Please ensure you follow all steps carefully for the most accurate assessment

  • Well-lit Environment

    Sit in a well-lit room to ensure clear visibility for facial analysis

  • Silent Environment

    Ensure you're in a quiet space so the system can record audio clearly

  • Working Camera

    Check that your device camera is functioning properly before starting

  • Honest Responses

    Provide accurate and truthful answers to all assessment questions

  • Audio Equipment

    Use headphones or ensure a quiet environment for voice analysis tasks

  • Uninterrupted Time

    Set aside 15-20 minutes to complete the assessment without distractions

  • Follow Instructions

    Read and follow each step of the assessment process carefully

  • Stay Relaxed

    Maintain a natural expression and calm demeanor throughout the assessment

Begin Assessment

The assessment takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete
